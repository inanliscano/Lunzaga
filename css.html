COMPUTER SYSTEM SERVICING




ICT: Computer Systems Servicing (CSS) NC II strand is a TVET program that provides students with the knowledge and skills necessary to install, configure, and maintain computer systems and networks.


It is ideal for individuals who have an interest in technology and want to pursue a career in the ICT industry.


The program provides practical skills, leads to a nationally-recognized certification, and offers career opportunities such as computer technician, IT support specialist, network administrator, technical support representative, and system administrator.
